About the Story

Yala is more than safari lore—it is a mosaic of lagoons, mangroves, and chena fields where painted storks, pelicans, and bee-eaters converge. This feature page highlights how postcards from the park narrate coexistence between pilgrims visiting Kataragama and the wildlife that drinks from the Menik River.
